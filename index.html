Community Hangar — Saturday Darts Tournament

Every Saturday at 19:00 we gather in the hangar for an informal darts tournament. Entry is anonymous, free, and open to everyone from the community — bring your friends, your helmet, and your game face.

  • When: Saturdays, 19:00 local time
  • Where: Community Hangar (behind the showroom)
  • Format: Single-elimination, best of 3 legs. Bracket posted at 18:50.
  • Entry: Anonymous, free. Sign-up at the door or drop a message via Telegram.
  • Vibe: Passion, clean lines, and fair play — bring energy, not drama.
